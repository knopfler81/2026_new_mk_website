Review

After a long and tiring trip, I arrived in Le Mans to support and try to meet my guitar hero… you can guess who — Mark Knopfler indeed.

Very early on Friday morning, I went to the ticket office to buy my precious pass, the one that would allow me to access Mark’s paddock.

After walking around for a while, admiring all the wonderful vintage cars, I finally discovered Mark’s beautiful Austin Healey from 1954, number 36.

Later, as I was leaving the paddock, I suddenly saw him… on a red scooter. Oh yes — that was him for sure! But he was in a hurry, no time to catch him.

Time passed, and there was still no sign of him. He was very discreet… almost impossible to find.

And then, just before going to bed, I finally met him. WOW. I almost didn’t believe it anymore.

I got nearly everything I had hoped for: an autograph on the back cover of my Strat, a couple of kisses, and a sweet smile.

The night was short, cold, and noisy… 24 hours of racing doesn’t make it easy to sleep.

The next afternoon, I had the chance to see him again. This time, he was surrounded by fans and didn’t have time for a picture with me. But to apologize, he blew me a kiss.

I left completely charmed.

Memory notes

  • Age: 25
  • With: Alone
  • Feeling: Excited, nervous and amazed
  • Highlight: Finally meeting Mark and getting an autograph and kisses
  • Strongest memory: Seeing him on the red scooter and then meeting him unexpectedly later that night
