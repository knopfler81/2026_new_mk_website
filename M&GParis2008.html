Review

It's time for me to share my best evening ever.

I attended the gig at Paris Bercy, and Ingrid and I were also invited to the Meet and Greet.

We had some trouble picking up the passes and then finding the entrance to the Meet and Greet, because the security staff didn’t seem to know where it was. We were really nervous and stressed, running all around the hall — and I was wearing high heels, gloups...

I was so afraid we would miss it because of that confusion. But in the end, we met a very kind man who took us to the right place.

There were around 17 people there, but I think only four of us were real fans. The others looked more like business guests.

Danny came first and shook our hands directly, and Mark did the same.

They played three songs together, and then Mark stopped for a moment to sign a few autographs and take pictures while the others were still playing.

As always, it was a pleasure to meet him, but this time there was so much emotion for me. I was shaking.

He signed my Kill to Get Crimson program and took a picture with me.

After that, we also saw Richard and Guy. Such sweet man too.

Memory notes

  • Age: 26
  • With: Ingrid
  • Feeling: Nervous, overwhelmed and incredibly happy
  • Highlight: Being invited to the Meet and Greet in Paris Bercy
  • Strongest memory: Shaking with emotion while Mark signed my program and took a picture with me
