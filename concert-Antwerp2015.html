Review

5th June 2015. After a long, long ride with Laurent, we finally arrived at the meeting point. It took us four hours to reach Antwerp, and almost one hour more to reach the Sportpaleis on the busy, busy Antwerp roads! We stopped there to rest and have food and drinks, then we just crossed the street to enter the Sportpaleis!

I got pretty shocked to see that we had to pay €0.40 to use the bathrooms. It is crazy — we pay quite a lot for a concert ticket, and then we pay to pee... Glad I only needed to go once... BTW...

Again I got a front-row seat on my favorite side of the stage, between Mike and Richard! Tonight's show was very good! But I can say that I missed the “French atmosphere”. People here are kind of quiet if you compare them to Paris shows!

Mark again skipped Speedway At Nazareth... sigh... but he was still in a very good mood, very smiling. The great interaction between the band members is still at its top; it's a pleasure to see them having fun!

The very same set list as the last night in Paris.

Again, it was a good show, even if at the very beginning the sound was not that good in my opinion, but it got better quickly!

There was only one standing ovation for Sultans of Swing, so as I said, Belgium is a quiet audience. I hope tomorrow's Dutch audience will be warmer!

My last sentence would be to thank all the people involved in making this tour possible for me this year... sharing rooms, car-pooling, Christmas presents, etc... I won't mention anybody, but people will recognize themselves ;)

Memory notes

  • Age: 34
  • With: MK friends
  • Feeling: Tired, happy and slightly nostalgic
  • Highlight: Front row again on my favorite side, between Mike and Richard
  • Strongest memory: The contrast with the Paris crowd, the quiet Belgian audience, and still seeing the band in such a great mood
