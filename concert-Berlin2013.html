Review

D-Day finally arrived, almost unspoilt! I had to mask all my MK friends from my Facebook and avoided forums, etc... I felt so “alone” for a couple of weeks.

I arrived in the middle of the afternoon in Berlin. I had left home at 7am though... the journey was pretty long... btw I had a good reason!

I first met Ingrid at Plus Hotel-Hostel, which was pretty close to the O2 Arena, and just next to the rest of the Berlin Wall. Although I recommend this hotel, it’s pretty cheap, clean, and comfortable. We went to have a late lunch with Maarten and Martina; Jeroen, Isaac and Martina joined us for a drink. Afterwards, we went to the venue.

A big venue, 15,000 seats I guess (like the POPBercy)... We were in the front row but on the very left. Do you know the feeling when “feelin’ so good” starts??? Your heart starts beating fast, shivers everywhere, and then a big man arrives on stage with the Union Jack jacket! Yeahh, PC comes and introduces MK every night!

And then all the guys come around and the show starts!!! What It Is for an opener is indeed a good song, but not as surprising as Border River was! But I like it anyway! And then many surprises in a row! On this tour you get more surprises every night, I guess!

Memory notes

  • Age: Newly 32
  • With: Ingrid and MK friends
  • Feeling: Excited and impatient
  • Highlight: Finally seeing the show in Berlin
  • Strongest memory: The rush when the intro began
