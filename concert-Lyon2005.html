Review

Finally the 5th of april 2005 came, the day I had been waiting for for years! (After the 2003 cancelled tour.) I arrived very early hoping to meet Mark and his musicians. But I missed them... I just saw from far Luke Brighty & Wiliam Toplay...the support band. I waited 7 hours in front of the Tony Garnier's hall before to see the man on the stage! I was so glad to see him again after four years of waiting, I jumped up and down in the audience because I was so happy .So much emotion, his new album Shangri La is a pearl Obviously I left the hall full with joy !! And I couldn't wait for the three following gigs I had booked.

Memory notes

  • Age: 23
  • With: Someboy I used to know
  • Feeling: Excited and overwhelmed
  • Highlight: New tour !
  • Strongest memory: 4 years waiting
