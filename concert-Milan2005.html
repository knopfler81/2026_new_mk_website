Review

On the 6th June 2005, I met Thomas my msn Austrian friend’s. It was hard to find each other with all the people waiting in front of the Forum. It was the 1st time we met! A really powerful audience, with very noisy Italian fans (they talk a lot)! Also a powerful Mark Knopfler! This evening was wonderful because Mark played Telegraph Road with his brand new Pensa guitar (that he got the same day). What a sound, what a beautiful instrument! Unfortunately the concert was very short compared to the others; they stopped playing after So far away.... a very short concert... 'sigh' The guys might have had to catch a plane... Anyway the evening ended well in a nice restaurant with Thomas! And the day after we had breakfast together and had lot of fun because of a little red car which couldn't manage to park for a while.... ( uhh ? Thomas?)
