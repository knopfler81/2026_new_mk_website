Shangri La

Paris, Bercy

19th April 2005

Previous concert Next concert

Review

I got up at 6:30 a.m. to catch the train from Saint-Étienne to Paris to see Mark Knopfler. I arrived very early at Bercy and waited for seven or eight hours. During that time, I met some lovely fellow fans and chatted with them a little.

Mark was absolutely amazing that evening — he was in great shape and gave a fantastic performance. We even had a wonderful “olé olé” moment, which was so much fun.

For the first time in my life, I dared to shout, “Mark, you’re the best, I love you!” And the most surprising thing was that he replied: “I love you too.” Wow. That was an unforgettable gig, maybe the best one I had seen so far.

Memory notes

  • Age: 23
  • With: On my own
  • Feeling: Excited and overwhelmed
  • Highlight: First time in Bercy
  • Strongest memory:MK replied me
