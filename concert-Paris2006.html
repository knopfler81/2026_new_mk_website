Review

12th June 2006, this date sounded pretty bad for personal reasons and I wasn't in my top form to attend this gig. But finally... Sometime you learn that music can make you feel better, change your mind, and definaltly I got blowed up! The duet was magical (even if i rather prefer MK alone on stages).
