Review

Paris – Accor Arena.

It’s always special to see Mark in Paris, especially in such a big venue. But this time… I was not well placed at all.

From where I was sitting, it felt far. Too far to really connect the way I like to. After being used to great seats for so many years, it was frustrating… one of those moments where you realize how much the placement changes everything.

I don’t even really remember who I was with that night… which says a lot about how I experienced the concert.

The Accor Arena is impressive, but it’s not always the best when you’re far from the stage. The sound and the atmosphere just don’t hit the same way.

Still, it’s Mark. The music is always there, and some moments still manage to go through, no matter where you are in the room.

And then came the encores…

That’s when everything changed a bit. The energy went up, the connection came back, and it finally felt like a real concert again. Not perfect, but enough to save the night.

In the end, not the best conditions, but still some good moments — especially at the end.
