Review

This concert started in a rather unexpected way... When the doors opened and we rushed toward the stage, I fell badly on the dry grass and hurt my knee.

I got back up limping, made my way to the front, then went to get some treatment before returning to my place again.

That painful entrance is probably my strongest memory of the day — along with the satisfaction of still making it to the front in the end.
