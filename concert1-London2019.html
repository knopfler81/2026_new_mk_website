Review

First night in London.

I travelled with Julien, his friend, and Marithée, and we met Gilda at Lyon airport. Once in London, we also met up again with the AFMK and my Dutch crew.

This year, the presale system was catastrophic... We were used to first come, first served, and this time it was a horrible lottery system.

So getting the front row on this first night felt even more precious than usual. After all these years of being spoiled with great seats, I knew how lucky we were this time.

Being back at the barrier changed everything immediately. The sound, the atmosphere, the closeness to the stage — everything felt right again.

It was such a relief to enjoy the show the way I love to: right in front, close enough to catch every little detail, every smile, every glance, every note.

And because this was Mark’s last tour, every concert felt even more special. You try to enjoy every moment a little more carefully, knowing these nights won’t come back.
