Review

Third concert in London and at last the last one in the UK.

THE BEST show so far!

Once again, my Dutch sister Ingrid got me the “best seat” in the house, on the right of the stage, just in front of Richard, Guy, and Mike!

Tonight was electric. When Paul Crockford arrived on stage to introduce Mark, we all stood up, until the end of the opening song Broken Bones! Clapping our hands, dancing, woowowing (I've just invented this word...).

Mark was absolutely perfect tonight! (Nice shirt.)

Actually there were several standing ovations... After Romeo and Juliet we launched the traditional French “Oé Oé Oé”. Mark played the game and started to put some music on it while we were singing the “Oé Oé Oé”. Then Sultans Of Swing started — we were on fire, we all stayed standing...

Some people from the back had arrived slowly to shoot some pictures, etc... It was getting hotter and dangerous to get our front row stolen! So I pushed my bag to the front and stepped forward to the security gate, for I think my earliest rush ever!! Security guys tried to kick us back to our seats but it was impossible to go back... The crowd had leaked from everywhere in the house!

I was happy to stand for almost half the concert, but I think it's a pity and I feel sorry for people who got their front row stolen by people from the back, as we all paid extra money to book such seats!

Btw, this concert was for me the best on this tour! I really wonder how it's gonna be tomorrow... I'm sure the security will be “stronger”... Let’s wait and see!

My last sentence would be to thank all the people involved in making this tour possible for me this year... sharing rooms, car-pooling, Christmas presents, etc... I won't mention anybody, but people will recognize themselves ;)

Memory notes

  • Age: 34
  • With: MK friends
  • Feeling: Excited, euphoric and unstoppable
  • Highlight: The whole crowd standing from Broken Bones and the French “Oé Oé Oé” with Mark joining in
  • Strongest memory: The electric atmosphere, the early rush to protect the front row, and feeling this was the best show of the tour so far
