Review

Another great morning and early afternoon with everybody having breakfast meal and drinks. Gig was fantastic I’ll never stop saying that... I was standing on the left side this time and my eyes were finally delighted !! No table ! And a very good view on MK’s beautiful hands ! Perfect sound again maybe not loud enought for some track but it’s just my opinion... (I’m a bit death). Perfect MK perfect Band !! Amazing men !! And lovely fans indeed!!

Memory notes

  • Age: 29
  • With: Many friends
  • Feeling: Tired, excited, grateful
  • Highlight: Getting second row despite the busy queue
  • Strongest memory: A perfect sunny concert day in Amsterdam, surrounded by friends, with Mark and the band playing like gods
