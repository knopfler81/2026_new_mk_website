Review

Second night in London.

I was with the same people again — Julien, his friend, Marithée, Gilda, the AFMK friends and of course my Dutch team.

Unfortunately, this time we were much farther from the stage. And when you have been used to front-row tickets for so many years, ending up far, far away really feels frustrating.

The presale system this year was such a disaster... We had been spoiled for years with the old first come, first served way, and the lottery system really changed everything — not in a good way.

Being that far from the stage changed the whole feeling of the evening for me. Of course the music was still there, the band was still great, and I was happy to be at the show... but it just didn’t feel the same.

So I waited for the encores, and then I rushed down to the front of the stage to finally enjoy the concert the way I wanted to.

That was probably the best moment of the night: finally getting closer and feeling connected again after spending most of the show too far away.
