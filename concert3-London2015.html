Review

Third concert in London and at last the last one in the UK.

As usual, we had a pre-meeting with fans at the Stanhope Arms Pub. A couple of drinks, a little food, and then we walked again to the RAH!

Again I had a front-row ticket! Thanks to Jeroen for getting me a good seat!! I had exactly the same seat as the day before, in the middle, a little to the left of Mark!

Ruth Moody opened the act again.

Then came Paul Crockford to introduce Mark Knopfler, and the show started :-)

Tonight's atmosphere was less warm than the previous night, but the band was at its best!

Same old set list, nothing has changed so far... I wonder if they will keep it this way. Btw, I wish I could hear more songs from the new album, which is just a gem. Anyway, I am happy with it all! I got lucky to be there.

Tonight's rush was a little later than yesterday — I mean later during the end of Telegraph Road — but I got surprised by some people rushing faster than me. Btw, I managed to be in the very front again. Actually, I got stuck between two guys and couldn't move anymore... I was bent in two on the stage, almost lying on it, and couldn't lift myself up at all... what an embarrassing position...

Mark shook some hands — well, when I say “shook,” it was not a real shake, I should say a quick “check.” I got lucky to have a quick check. He also gave some picks to other people! This year Mark interacts much more with the audience than usual.

My last sentence would be to thank all the people involved in making this tour possible for me this year... sharing rooms, car-pooling, Christmas presents, etc... I won't mention anybody, but people will recognize themselves ;) (yes, it's going to be the same sentence for every page of this tour...)

Memory notes
