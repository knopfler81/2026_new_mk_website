Mark
In Summer
Long Ago

“Mark In Summer Long Ago…” refers to the very first time I saw Mark Knopfler live on stage, on July 9, 1996, and to his beautiful song A Night In Summer Long Ago, which I used to listen to with my beloved grandmother.

This website is my way of sharing my passion for Mark Knopfler’s music with you. Here, you will find reviews and photos from the concerts I have attended, along with memories of the occasions when I had the privilege of meeting the man himself. I hope you enjoy your visit.
